Support

For support and instructions of creating and publishing surveys and reports, please contact us as follows:

  • Log into Webropol and open your survey on edit/collect answers/follow up/reporting tab.
  • Open the 'Contact Support' form on the right side of the page.
  • Check the box under 'I grant Webropol support permission to access my user account' to allow Service Desk to view your survey/report.
