FAQ

  • I have not used Webropol Survey&Reporting yet, where can I get help?
    Webropol Survey&Reporting has an improved design and additional features to make your surveys even better, and is easier to use! On the Apps menu you will find the W-videos section, where you can find free videos to get you started. You can also purchase personal training videos from the W-store, which are available for 3 months from the date of purchase.
  • Where can I find my Events registration form?
    Events forms are currently found under the Apps dropdown menu, in Events Registration. From Apps dropdown menu you can also go to W-Store, BI VIEW and Webropol Survey&Reporting.
  • What is Webropol BI VIEW?
    BI VIEW is a reporting portal developed by Webropol, where you can easily create dashboards and visualize your data according to your specific needs. In addition to attaching graphs to Webropol reports, you can also attach tables, pictures, and videos and share them within your organisation, with stakeholders, or publicly.
